## Hi, I'm Marcin 👋

Welcome to my blog.

This space is where I document my journey into cybersecurity — from the basics to more advanced topics over time. I'm not an expert (yet), but I strongly believe that sharing the process is just as valuable as sharing the results.

---

## Why this blog?

I created this blog mainly for two reasons:

- To **track my own progress**
- To **build a personal knowledge base**

Instead of scattered notes, I want everything in one place — structured, searchable, and useful both for me and anyone who might be on a similar path.

---

## What you can expect here

This blog will include:

### 🔹 Hack The Box write-ups
Step-by-step walkthroughs of machines I solve, including:
- enumeration process
- thought process
- mistakes and lessons learned

---

### 🔹 Notes & cheat sheets
Things I don’t want to forget, for example:
- Nmap usage
- enumeration techniques
- useful commands and workflows

Basically — my personal "go-to" reference.

---

### 🔹 Learning journey & progress
I’ll also share:
- what I’m currently learning
- what challenges I’m facing
- how my approach evolves over time

No fake perfection — just real progress.

---

## My approach

I focus on:
- understanding *why* something works  
- building repeatable processes  
- learning by doing (not just reading)

---

## Final thoughts

This blog is not about being an expert.

It’s about:
- consistency  
- curiosity  
- and getting better step by step  

If you’re on a similar journey — feel free to follow along.

---

Thanks for being here.
